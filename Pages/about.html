Organization Overview

PE.T (Peer Tutoring Program) supports university students struggling with technical and software development subjects by providing peer learning opportunities and technical assistance.

Target Audience: University students seeking expert peer guidance.

Goals and Objectives

Primary Goals

  • Provide a platform for students to connect with knowledgeable peer tutors.
  • Offer comprehensive resources and study materials for academic success.
  • Facilitate clear communication and scheduling between tutors and students.

Objectives

  • Increase student engagement and improve academic performance through accessible tutoring.
  • Develop a user-friendly website interface that encourages frequent use.
  • Ensure accessibility and inclusivity for diverse learners, including those with disabilities.

Program Analysis and Insights
