Our Tutoring Services

The PE.T program offers a wide range of peer-led academic support services to help university students succeed in technical and software development subjects.

Available Services

  • 1-on-1 Peer Tutoring – Personalized sessions with a qualified student tutor.
  • Group Study Sessions – Collaborative learning in small peer groups.
  • Code Review & Debugging – Help with understanding and improving your code.
  • Project Guidance – Support with capstone or class projects.
  • Exam Preparation – Focused help for midterms, finals, and technical assessments.

Special Events & Workshops

We occasionally host workshops and seminars on topics like:

  • Intro to Python or JavaScript
  • Version Control (Git/GitHub)
  • How to Build a Portfolio Website
  • Technical Interview Preparation
