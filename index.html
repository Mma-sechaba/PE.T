Learn Together, Succeed Together: Peer Tutoring

Let us get busy squad!

The peer tutoring program focuses on enhancing technical and software development through collaborative learning and peer support. It is targeted at university students who are struggling or looking to improve their coding skills and academic marks. Its main goal is to foster a supportive community, improve academic performance, and develop leadership skills among peer tutors.
